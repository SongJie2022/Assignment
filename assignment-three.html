Introduction

Background information on the project

Hong Kong, a city with a rich cultural heritage, food culture, and modern lifestyle, attracts many students, especially mainland students, to study in Hong Kong every year. When mainland students arrive in Hong Kong, they have a strong interest in the city and a desire to go out and explore.

You can always see people asking on Little Red Book and in WeChat groups if anyone wants to go hiking or to go to an amusement park together. In real life, we are also often faced with situations where we want to go out to participate in some activities, but our friends do not have time or are not interested in them.

Unable to find friends, we are faced with the choice of going alone or abandoning our plans, which already has a negative impact on our experience. Or, as mentioned above, we could consider platforms such as Little Red Book to find others who might be interested in the activity, but because of the diversity of content on the platforms and safety reasons, the efficiency and results may not be as satisfactory.

The market needs a specific platform to help people find partners to go out with. And that is the reason why I want to design this website. For more information, you can refer here.
